what are our interests ?

Education

production of free online content and books, organization of trainings and competitions and support of Moroccan brillant students in mathematics

Research

organization of scientific research in applied and pure mathematics and collection of statistics about the situation of mathematics in Morocco

Pop Science

vulgarization of mathematics for the public audience and connect the Moroccan mathematical community

Art & Media

sensibilize people by the importance and the beauty of mathematics with cinema, painting, sculpture and other art forms
